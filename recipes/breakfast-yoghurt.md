### Ingredients
- Handful of cashew nuts
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 4 tbsp Greek yoghurt
- 1 apple


### Steps
Place the cashew nuts and chia seeds in a bowl. Add the yoghurt then mix thoroughly. Chop the apple and mix through.
